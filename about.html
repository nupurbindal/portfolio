I’m a first year Masters in Information student at the University of Michigan with a focus on
Human-Computer Interaction. With an undergraduate degree in Math and an undying love for Art
and Design, I found my equilibrium in UX Design - a field that lets me explore the creative
within a technical realm. As a UX Designer, I’m committed to building accessible and inclusive
systems that are flexible and allow all members to feel welcome on a platform.




Here are two of my favorite things:

Tea

My enthusiasm for tea is fairly recent. I had my first cup of green tea maybe 3 years ago and the excitment hasn't faded since. I now make it a point to always have atleast 7 different varieties of tea on hand. Flavours count. Despite my affection for this well-loved beverage, I do not like my tea with milk.

Photography

I've been taking pictures since I in middle school. It started with an attempt to avoid being in front of the camera myself. Eventually, it developed into a passion that has grown with time. The biggest takeaway I've had from my experience as a photography, as cliche as it sounds, is that the best camera in the world is the one you have with you.
